    Research Paper and Term Paper Comparison

    Both research paper and term paper are among the significant academic assignments that every student should know how to write. However, each of these papers has unique characteristics that should be followed. Here is a brief table of differences which was completed for your convenience and better understanding of each type.

    Research Paper:

    • Should cite interviews, observations, questionnaires, surveys
    • Research paper is based on the hypothesis that should be proved or declined
    • Content concerns the research of the specific problem/hypothesis
    • Depends on the difficulty of research, but research paper writing service can complete it within a few hours.
    • Research paper should be 500-1000 words long
    • Does not count towards the final grade

    Term Paper:

    • Main sources are textbooks, academic papers, journals
    • Term paper has a specific thesis statement that should be supported
    • Aims to demonstrate the knowledge of the specific subject/cours
    • Should be completed during the term or half of the term, but term paper writing service can complete it within a few days.
    • Term paper should be 3000-5000 words long
    • Influences the final grade

    Writing a research paper in college is practically the same as cooking. Your research materials are your ingredients and you have to find a method to prepare and cook your materials into a delicious meal (finished paper).

    There are three crucial points to consider when seeking research paper writing service:

    • Writers should be experienced, so that the points, analysis, critiques, and/or arguments are presented in an organized manner.

    To present a delicious essay, you need great materials and sources, an organized writing style, and an experienced writer.
